ABOUT US

The Ming's diner family is synonymous with a commitment to quality and a high degree of professionalism by using state of the art equipment in the manufacture of sweets, savouries, snacks and myriad varieties of food. It is the taste of our food, coupled with our commitment to hygiene that makes us who we are today- a brand renowned for our authenticity. Today, ming's diner is the most recognised restaurant in Chennai, cherished by many for our bountiful food at affordable prices!
